1. Introduction and Motivation
CompoundChat is a WhatsApp bot that unlocks Compound's DeFi protocols for the world's most widely-used communication platform. By integrating Compound's lending and borrowing functionalities directly within WhatsApp where 2+ billion users already communicate daily we remove the friction of downloading new apps or navigating complex interfaces, empowering everyday users across Africa, Latin America, and Asia to access secure, decentralized finance through simple text commands.
Our goal is simple: expand Compound's distribution channel by leveraging WhatsApp's ubiquitous reach in high-growth markets, thereby driving genuine new user engagement from demographics currently underserved by traditional DeFi interfaces.
Why Messaging for DeFi:
Universal reach - 2+ billion active users globally, with highest concentration in emerging markets
Zero friction onboarding - No app downloads, no browser extensions, works on any device
Trusted environment - Already used for banking and payments in many countries
Low data consumption - Critical for users on limited mobile data plans in Africa and Asia
Natural conversation flow - Makes complex DeFi operations intuitive through chat-based interactions
Built-in virality - Social sharing and referrals happen organically within users' existing contact networks
2. What It Does
CompoundChat is a conversational bot that makes DeFi as simple as sending a message. Key features include:
Chat-Based DeFi Operations
Users interact with Compound through natural language commands:
"Balance" - Check lending positions and yields
"Supply 100 USDC" - Deposit assets to earn interest
"Borrow 50 USDC" - Take out loans against collateral
"Markets" - View all available Compound V3 markets
"Help" - Guided onboarding for DeFi newcomers
Smart Wallet Integration
Non-custodial wallet creation via messaging
Secure key management with recovery phrases sent via encrypted messages
Support for existing wallet imports (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, Rabby)
Biometric authentication for transactions
Multi-signature support for shared accounts
Real-Time Notifications
Interest earned updates (daily/weekly summaries)
Collateral ratio alerts to prevent liquidations
APY changes in your active markets
Security notifications for large transactions
Market opportunity alerts
Mobile Money Integration
Seamless connection to Africa's mobile money infrastructure:
M-Pesa (Kenya, Tanzania, South Africa, Ghana)
MTN Mobile Money (Uganda, Ghana, Nigeria, Cameroon, Ivory Coast)
Users can go from local currency → stablecoins → Compound in minutes, unlocking DeFi for the 500+ million mobile money users across Africa.
Social Features
Refer friends via messaging contacts
Share yields with privacy controls
Group savings pools for communities
Status updates for achievements
Leaderboards for top earners (opt-in)

Technical Risks & Mitigations
Platform API Changes: Build abstraction layer to easily swap messaging platforms if needed; maintain compliance with platform guidelines
Smart Contract Security: All interactions with Compound V3 use audited, standard patterns; no custom smart contracts needed; independent security review in Milestone 3
Key Management: Military-grade encryption for wallet keys; user-controlled recovery phrases; optional integration with hardware wallets for power users
Network Reliability: Optimistic transaction queuing; graceful degradation on poor connections; SMS fallback for critical operations

No Financial Advice: Purely technical tool for DeFi access; clear disclaimers
KYC/AML: Handled by mobile money partners where required by local regulations (most African countries have clear mobile money KYC rules)

Data Privacy: GDPR-compliant for European users; compliance with Kenya's Data Protection Act and similar African regulations
